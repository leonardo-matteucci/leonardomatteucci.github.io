Biography

Leonardo Matteucci is a composer exploring inner corporeality, the mechanics of bodily articulation, and the tactility of acoustic-electronic hybridisation.

He studied composition with Giorgio Colombo Taccani in Turin (2019-2021) and Marco Momi in Fermo (2021-2023), and is currently pursuing a Master's degree under Franck Bedrossian at Kunstuniversität Graz.

Studies

  • 2023– – Master’s in Composition with Franck Bedrossian, Kunstuniversität Graz
  • 2021–2023 – Composition studies with Marco Momi, Fermo Conservatory
  • 2019–2021 – Composition studies with Giorgio Colombo Taccani, Turin Conservatory

Influences

Drawing from corporeal practices and electronic experimentation, his work seeks a tactile, physical engagement with sound.
