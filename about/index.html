About

Leonardo Matteucci is a composer exploring inner corporeality, the mechanics of bodily articulation, and the tactility of acoustic-electronic hybridisation.

He studied composition with Giorgio Colombo Taccani in Turin (2019–2021) and Marco Momi in Fermo (2021–2023), and is currently pursuing a Master’s degree under Franck Bedrossian at the Kunstuniversität Graz.
