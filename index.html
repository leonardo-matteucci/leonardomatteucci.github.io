About

Born in Italy in 2000, Leonardo Matteucci studies composition in Turin with Giorgio Colombo Taccani and in Fermo with Marco Momi and participates as an active member in masterclasses/workshops with Marco Stroppa, Salvatore Sciarrino, Francesca Verunelli, Zeno Baldi, Clara Iannotta, Pierluigi Billone, Oscar Bianchi, Mauro Lanza, mdi ensemble, Francesco Dillon and Michele Marco Rossi. His music has been performed by Opificio Sonoro, Quartetto Maurice and Collettivo_21, among others.
