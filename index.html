About

Born in Perugia, Italy, in 2000, Leonardo Matteucci is a composition student who studied in Turin with Giorgio Colombo Taccani from 2019 to 2021, and in Fermo with Marco Momi from 2021 to 2023. He is now pursuing his Master’s in Composition under Franck Bedrossian at the University of Music and Performing Arts Graz. He has actively participated in masterclasses with composers such as Marco Stroppa, Franck Bedrossian, Clara Iannotta, Pierluigi Billone, Francesca Verunelli, and Mauro Lanza. His works have been performed by ensembles such as Opificio Sonoro, Quartetto Maurice, and Collettivo_21.
