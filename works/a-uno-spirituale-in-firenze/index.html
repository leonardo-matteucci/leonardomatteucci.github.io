A uno spirituale in Firenze2021

5′

  • Violin I
  • Violin II
  • Viola
  • Cello
  • Electronics:
    fixed media (mono)
    • no instrumental amplification required

Premiere: 29 October 2021, Serate Musicali, Turin Conservatory

Five aphorisms inspired by an excerpt from the homonymous letter by Saint Catherine of Siena, addressed "to a devotee who was scandalized by her abstinences." This letter is taken from the second volume of a collection edited by Niccolò Tommaseo and published in Florence in 1860 by G. Barbèra.

  1. «[…] e prego Dio e pregherò, che mi dia grazia che in quest’atto del mangiare io viva come le altre creature […]»
    “[…] and I pray and will continue to pray to God to grant me the grace to live, in this act of eating, as other creatures do […]”
  2. «[…] e Dio che per singolarissima grazia m’abbia fatto correggere il vizio della gola […]»
    “[…] and God who, through a most singular grace, has allowed me to correct the vice of gluttony […]”
  3. «[…] Io per me non so che altro rimedio ponermici, … se non ch’io prego voi che preghiate quella somma eterna Verità […]»
    “[…] As for myself, I do not know what other remedy I can apply, except to ask you to pray to that supreme eternal Truth […]”
  4. «E io son certa, che la bontà di Dio non spregierà le vostre orazioni.»
    “And I am certain that the goodness of God will not despise your prayers.”
  5. «[…] che mi dia grazia, […] che mi faccia prendere il cibo, se gli piace.»
    “[…] that He may grant me the grace […] to partake of food, if it pleases Him.”

A uno spirituale in Firenze is dedicated to the memory of Carla Massini
