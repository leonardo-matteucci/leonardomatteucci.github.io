Bodylines (2023)

Duration: 7′30″

Instrumentation: Violin (+ Nebulizer tube); Piccolo (+ ACME Whistles® Silent Dog Whistle 535); fixed-media stereo electronics – 3 audio exciters (each with a stereo digital amplifier), 1 contact microphone, 1 lavalier microphone.

A transformed body needs a new representation: what persists are the lines in its movements; there is no musical semantics, only the need to move, to breathe within it.
