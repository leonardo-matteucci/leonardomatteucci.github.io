Internal (2023)

Duration: 10′

Instrumentation: Flute (with B foot); Clarinet in B-flat; Vibraphone (+ Crotales: F#6 G#6 A6 B6); Guitar; Piano (+ EBow); Accordion; Violin; Cello.

Commissioned by and dedicated to Opificio Sonoro

Premiere: 11 May 2023, Festival Orizzonti, Perugia

Establishing a relationship with what we cannot directly interact with: our bodily interior, the joints that emerge from within, contracting, bending, compressing; to better control, anticipate, or even break them, their flexions, and finally stretch them out.
