Internal (2023) [10.00]

for ensemble (8) and electronics

Commissioned by and dedicated to Opificio Sonoro

World premiere: 11 May 2023, Festival Orizzonti, Perugia

In Internal there is my first attempt to establish a relationship with what we cannot directly interact with, our interior, bodily, the joints that emerge from within, contracting, bending, compressing; in order to better control, anticipate, or even break them, their flexions, and finally stretch them out.
